---
title: Eve and the Church
---
The first marriage recorded in Scripture was not merely a historical event, but a divine picture of a far greater relationship—**Christ and His Church**. 
As Eve was formed for Adam, so the Church has been called, formed, and prepared for Christ.

| Eve                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | The Church                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **In Adam**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | **In Christ** (Ephesians 1:4 - "According as he hath chosen us in him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy and without blame before him in love:")                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Taken from Adam**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | **From Christ** (Ephesians 4:15–16 - "But speaking the truth in love, may grow up into him in all things, which is the head, even Christ: From whom the whole body fitly joined together and compacted by that which every joint supplieth, according to the effectual working in the measure of every part, maketh increase of the body unto the edifying of itself in love.") |
| **Built from Adam's side** (Genesis 2:21–22 - "And the LORD God caused a deep sleep to fall upon Adam, and he slept: and he took one of his ribs, and closed up the flesh instead thereof; And the rib, which the LORD God had taken from man, made he a woman, and brought her unto the man.") | **Built from Christ's pierced side** (John 19:34; Acts 20:28 - But one of the soldiers with a spear pierced his side, and forthwith came there out blood and water."<br><br>"Take heed therefore unto yourselves, and to all the flock, over the which the Holy Ghost hath made you overseers, to feed the church of God, which he hath purchased with his own blood.")         |
| **For Adam**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | **For Christ** (2 Corinthians 11:2: "For I am jealous over you with godly jealousy: for I have espoused you to one husband, that I may present you as a chaste virgin to Christ.")                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Presented to Adam**                                                                                                                                       | **Presented to Christ** (Ephesians 5:27: "That he might present it to himself a glorious church, not having spot, or wrinkle, or any such thing; but that it should be holy and without blemish.")                                                                                                                                                                              |
