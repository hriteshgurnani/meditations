---
title: Treasures in Christ
---

> "Search the scriptures; for in them ye think ye have eternal life: and they are they which testify of me."  
> — **John 5:39**

A collection of Christ-centered devotionals and gleanings from the Word of God, with the desire that every study might lead us to know, love, and worship the Lord Jesus Christ more deeply.


- **Devotionals** — Short meditations on the Person, Work, and Glory of the Lord Jesus Christ.
- **Gleanings** — Scriptural studies, types, and practical lessons gathered from the Word of God.

> "For of Him, and through Him, and to Him, are all things: to whom be glory for ever. Amen."  
> — **Romans 11:36**
